Recipe for Pho

This is the classic Vietnamese Pho recipe, a deliciously hearty noodle soup dish

Ingredients

  • 200g of pasta of your choice
  • 300g of ground beef
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • 1 Onion
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta for 8 minutes
  2. Roughly chop the tomatoes
  3. Dice the onion
  4. Put 1 tbsp olive oil on a heated pan
  5. Add onion
  6. Add tomatoes
  7. Add ground beef and fry until brown
  8. Add salt
  9. Add some tomato sauce
  10. Put over pasta and voila
